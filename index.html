Three steps. One of them is yours.

Install it, tell it one number, and go back to your life. The pills handle themselves.

1

Tell it what you make

Hourly rate or yearly salary, in any of 48 currencies. It guesses yours from your timezone and you can correct it. The number is saved on your device and goes nowhere, ever.

2

It does rude arithmetic

On shop pages, every price is divided by your rate, right where it stands. Sale prices win over crossed out theater, subscriptions get marked per month, and foreign currencies convert with daily rates.

3

You buy on purpose

Some things are absolutely worth the hours. Plenty of things are not. The whole trick is being able to tell which is which before the checkout button, not after.

It knows when to shut up.

A wrong badge is worse than no badge, so the engine is picky on purpose. Marketing theater, UI furniture and your own dashboards get nothing.
